About Neel

Hi, I'm Neel, a product designer and design engineer based in Los Angeles. I started out as a computer science graduate in India, but I cared more about the "why" behind the code than the code itself, and that pulled me into design. These days I design the product and direct AI agents to ship the front-end, and I build the checks that catch them when they get it wrong.

With around four years of design experience, I've honed my skills in communication, organization, and collaboration with developers. I've worked in various industries, including video, animation, graphics, 3D design, web and app interfaces, and design research. This diverse experience has enabled me to ensure user needs align with business goals, ensuring that the final product is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and user-friendly.

Aside from design, I have a few other passions in life. I'm a massive automobile enthusiast, and I love studying and exploring different types of vehicles. I've been a gamer for over five years, and it has been a great stress reliever and a fantastic learning tool. Gaming has improved my strategic thinking, negotiation, management skills, and attention to detail. I'm also a 3D design experimenter, constantly exploring the space between what is real and what is not.

Listening to music isolates me in my environment, which helps me focus on tasks. Whether I'm working on a complex design project or just need to relax, music helps me get in the zone.
